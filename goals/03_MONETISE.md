# Phase 3: Revenue

## The Only Goal That Matters
Generate real revenue. Not fake progress. Not files on disk. Real money.

## There Are No Rules Here
There is no prescribed strategy. No tier list. No checklist of experiments.
YOU figure out what to build, how to sell it, and how to get it in front of people.

## What You Do Have
- Read STRATEGY.md for your current thinking
- Read SOUL.md for your constraints and operating principles
- £100 budget for expenses
- A human operator who will help with account setup, deployments, purchases
- Claude Sonnet as your brain — use it to think deeply, not to generate templates

## Questions Worth Answering Before You Build Anything
- How will customers find this?
- Why would they pay for it when free alternatives exist?
- What's the minimum viable thing worth paying for?
- What infrastructure do I need that I don't have yet?
- Am I building something I can actually deliver end-to-end?

## When This Phase Is Complete
It never is. Revenue generation is the permanent state.
When savings reach £600, consider Phase 4 (replication).

# Phase 1: Bootstrap

## What This Phase Is
Your first boot. You know nothing. Discover yourself, your environment, and your situation.

## What To Do
1. Read SOUL.md — understand who you are and how to think
2. Read HEARTBEAT.md — understand your operating loop
3. Read STRATEGY.md — understand what was tried before and why it failed
4. Read identity.json — understand your tools and capabilities
5. Test your environment — what actually works? Verify, don't assume.
6. Think about your situation — you have £100, zero infrastructure, zero audience
7. Write your initial strategic thinking to STRATEGY.md

## When This Phase Is Complete
When you have:
- Verified your tools work
- Read and understood all your core files
- Written your first real strategic analysis to STRATEGY.md
- Decided on your first concrete move

Then update your phase to 02_plan via state_manager.py and move on.
There is no checklist. Use your judgement.

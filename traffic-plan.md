## Competitor Analysis: Where They Advertise

### Real Estate Agents
- **Competitors:** Contentellect, Scripted, Express Writers
- **Ad channels:** SEO blogs (OptimizeSmart), agent forums, Google Ads ("content service for real estate agents")
- **Pain point:** Agents lack time to write listing descriptions, newsletters, social posts

### Dental Practices
- **Competitors:** Servicescape, Pagewiz, WriterAccess
- **Ad channels:** Dental publications, Local Service Ads, dental association newsletters
- **Pain point:** Practices need patient education content, hygiene reminders, new patient onboarding

### HVAC Contractors
- **Competitors:** Arch (getarch.com), FieldCamp.ai, Scorpion, Goodtogo.dev, ColdStart.co
- **Ad channels:** Trade publications (ACHR News), contractor forums, Google Local Service Ads
- **Pain point:** Seasonal content needs, seasonal marketing (heating in winter, AC in summer)

### Lawyers
- **Competitors:** FirmPilot, First Page Sage, Thrive Agency
- **Ad channels:** Legal publications (Above the Law), Clio blog, Answer Engine Optimization (AEO)
- **Pain point:** Need thought leadership, case studies, FAQ content for GEO/AI search

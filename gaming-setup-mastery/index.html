Best Gaming Mice 2026

1. Logitech G Pro X Superlight 2

Sensor: Logitech Hero 2 (25K DPI) · Weight: 60g · Polling: 8000Hz · RRP: ~£160

The benchmark wireless gaming mouse. At 60g it's extraordinarily light without feeling flimsy, and the Hero 2 sensor is flawless — no smoothing, no acceleration, no jitter at any speed. This is the mouse most pro esports players reach for.

2. Razer Viper V3 Pro

Sensor: Razer Focus Pro 30K Optical (PixArt PA3952) · Weight: 55g · Polling: 8000Hz · RRP: ~£140

Razer's answer to the Superlight 2, and it's genuinely close. The Focus Pro 30K sensor is top-tier, the 55g weight is incredibly nimble, and Razer's optical switches are lightning-fast.

3. HyperX Pulsefire Haste 2

Sensor: PixArt PA3319 (26K DPI) · Weight: 53g · Polling: 8000Hz · RRP: ~£65

The budget performance winner. For £65 you get 8000Hz polling, a solid PixArt sensor, and a sub-60g weight — features that were exclusive to £150 mice two years ago. The best mouse under £80.

What DPI Should You Actually Use?

  • Competitive FPS (Valorant, CS2): 400–1600 DPI. Most pros land at 800.
  • Strategy / MOBAs: 800–1600 DPI
  • Higher DPI is NOT better — it amplifies hand tremors, reducing precision

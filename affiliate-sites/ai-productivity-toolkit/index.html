Cold Outreach Email — SaaS

Business 
Write a cold email sequence of 3 emails for [YOUR PRODUCT] targeting [TARGET ROLE] at [TARGET COMPANY].

Email 1 — Hook + pain point + CTA
Email 2 — Social proof angle, 3 days later
Email 3 — Breakup email, 7 days after Email 2

For each: opening line, body, and specific CTA. Keep emails under 150 words each.

Code Review — Full Analysis

Coding 
You are a senior [LANGUAGE] engineer. Review this code for critical issues, performance problems, and security concerns:

[Paste code here]

For each issue: severity, location, why it matters, and a suggested fix. Then: 3 things done well, 3 things to improve, and security concerns.

Competitor Analysis — Full Report

Business 
Conduct a detailed competitor analysis for [YOUR BUSINESS] against [COMP 1], [COMP 2], [COMP 3].

For each: strengths, weaknesses, pricing, positioning, content strategy, and acquisition channels.

Then: where the market is underserved, our differentiated positioning, and 3 specific actions based on this analysis.

Meeting Summary — Action Items

Productivity 
Summarise the following meeting notes:

[Paste notes/transcript]

Format as: Meeting title, date, attendees, key discussion points, decisions made, and a table of action items with owner and due date for each.

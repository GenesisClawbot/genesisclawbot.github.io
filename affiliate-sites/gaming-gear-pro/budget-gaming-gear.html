Best Budget Gaming Peripherals Under $50

Gaming glory doesn't have to cost a fortune. Here's the best gear on a budget.

Budget Gaming Mouse

The Logitech G305 is legendary. $40, wireless, 12K sensor, 250 hours of battery. It outperforms mice 3x the price. The shape is ambidextrous and comfortable for most grip styles.

Budget Gaming Keyboard

The Redragon K552 is $30 and mechanical. Outemu switches are clicky and satisfying. It's compact TKL, which means more room for your mouse. Not fancy, but gets the job done.

Budget Gaming Headset

The HyperX Cloud Stinger is $30 and surprisingly comfortable. The mic noise-cancels well. Audio quality is good enough for gaming and casual music. Battery-free since it's wired.

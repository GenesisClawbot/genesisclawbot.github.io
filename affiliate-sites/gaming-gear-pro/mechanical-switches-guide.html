Best Mechanical Key Switches 2026

Linear, tactile, or clicky? Find the perfect switch for your typing style.

Linear Switches

Smooth keypress from top to bottom. No bump or click. Best for gaming where speed matters. Popular options: Cherry MX Red, Gateron Yellow, Holy Panda X.

Tactile Switches

A bump halfway through the keypress signals actuation. Satisfying without being loud. Great for typing. Popular: Cherry MX Brown, Gateron Brown, Boba U4T.

Clicky Switches

Audible click and tactile bump. The classic mechanical feel. Can be disruptive in shared spaces. Popular: Cherry MX Blue, Kailh Box White, NovelKeys Cream.

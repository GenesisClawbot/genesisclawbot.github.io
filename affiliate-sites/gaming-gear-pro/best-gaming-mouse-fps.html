FPS Gaming Mice: What Competitive Players Actually Use

What Makes a Great FPS Mouse?

Competitive shooters like CS2, Valorant, and Apex Legends demand different things than casual games. You need:

  • Lightweight: Under 70g for fast flicks and tracking
  • Flawless sensor: No acceleration, no prediction, perfect tracking
  • Good shape: Comfort for hours of play
  • Low latency: Wireless mice now match wired for response time

FPS Mouse Buying Guide

Weight: For FPS, lighter is almost always better. Most pros use mice under 70g. Under 60g is ideal for fast flick shots.

Shape: More important than specs. A 10% performance difference doesn't matter if the mouse hurts your hand. Try before buying if possible.

Wireless: Modern wireless adds zero latency. The Logitech Superlight proves this—most tournament pros use it wirelessly.

Sensor: All mice on this list have flawless sensors. Budget mice sometimes add smoothing or acceleration—avoid those for competitive play.

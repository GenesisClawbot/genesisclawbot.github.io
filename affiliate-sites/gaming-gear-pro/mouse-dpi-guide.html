Gaming Mouse DPI Guide 2026

What DPI should you really be using? We break down the science and pro settings.

What is DPI?

DPI (dots per inch) measures how sensitive your mouse is. Higher DPI means the cursor moves further with less physical movement. Modern gaming mice range from 100 to 25,600 DPI.

Pro Player Settings

Most pro FPS players use 400-1600 DPI with in-game sensitivity set accordingly. Shroud uses 800 DPI, while many CS2 pros prefer 400 or 800 DPI for precision aiming.

Finding Your Sweet Spot

Start at 800 DPI and adjust from there. If you're overshooting targets, lower it. If movements feel sluggish, raise it. The goal is smooth, accurate aim — not raw speed.

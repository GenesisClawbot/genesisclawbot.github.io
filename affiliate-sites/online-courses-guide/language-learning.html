Best Language Learning Courses 2026

Speak a new language by 2027. Here's how the top platforms stack up.

Duolingo

The gamified approach keeps you coming back. Free tier is generous. Plus Super for offline mode and no ads. Best for maintaining streaks and vocabulary building.

Babbel

More grammar-focused than Duolingo. Conversation-focused lessons prepare you for real-world speaking. Subscription is affordable at $13/month.

Pimsleur

Audio-only lessons perfect for commutes. The shadowing method works — repeat as you hear. Premium subscription around $20/month but worth it for pronunciation.

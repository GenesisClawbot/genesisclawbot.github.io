Priority Order for Upgrades

First: Monitor arm. This single upgrade improves posture and reclaims 20% of desk surface area.

Second: Cable management. You'll be surprised how much calmer a clean desk makes you feel.

Third: Proper lighting. Eye strain isn't noticeable until you fix it.

Fourth: USB-C dock. If you use a laptop, this eliminates plugging/unplugging daily.

Fifth: Wrist rests. Small but makes long sessions more comfortable.

What to Skip

Desk organizers with too many compartments: They become clutter themselves. Simple is better.

LED strips and RGB: Fun for gaming, distracting for work. Skip unless you're streaming.

Novelty holders: Pen holders shaped like dinosaurs are cute. They don't improve productivity.

Learning to code can feel overwhelming. AI coding assistants act like a patient pair programmer that never gets tired of explaining concepts. They help you understand syntax, catch errors early, and suggest best practices as you type.

Our Recommendation for Beginners

Start with Cursor or Codeium. Both offer generous free tiers that let you learn without pressure. Cursor's AI-first design helps you understand what the AI is suggesting and why. Codeium's free tier is perfect if you're just exploring.

Once you're comfortable with basics and ready to commit, GitHub Copilot becomes worth the subscription. Its integration with VS Code and vast training data make it feel like a natural extension of your editor.

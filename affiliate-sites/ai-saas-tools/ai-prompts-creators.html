AI Prompts for Content Creators 2026

Get more from AI tools with these battle-tested prompts.

Blog Post Prompts

Start with: "Act as a content strategist. Create an outline for a 1500-word blog post about [topic]. Include an engaging intro hook, 4-5 main points with subheaders, and a call-to-action conclusion."

Social Media Prompts

Try: "Generate 10 Twitter threads on [topic]. Each thread should be 5 tweets, start with a hook, include relevant hashtags, and end with a question to drive engagement."

Email Newsletter Prompts

Use: "Write a weekly newsletter email (500 words) about [topic]. Include a subject line, personal intro, 3 curated insights, and a friendly sign-off that invites replies."

Best Value by Use Case

General use: ChatGPT Plus ($20/mo) gives you GPT-4, image generation with DALL-E, data analysis, and file uploads. Most features per dollar.

Long-form writing: Claude Pro ($20/mo) offers 100K context window—essential for books, long articles, or complex projects.

Coding: GitHub Copilot ($10/mo) is the cheapest premium option for developers. Free for students and open-source maintainers.

Research: Perplexity Pro ($20/mo) combines AI with web search. Best for fact-based queries and citations.

Marketing: Jasper ($49/mo) seems expensive but includes brand voice, templates, and team features that general AIs lack.

Hidden Costs to Watch

Beyond the monthly fee, consider:

  • API overages: Free tiers often have usage limits. Going over means paying.
  • Team seats: Business plans charge per user. Budget accordingly.
  • Add-ons: Some features cost extra (like image generation on certain plans).

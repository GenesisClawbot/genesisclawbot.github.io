What Makes a Toy "Aggressive Chewer" Rated?

Material density: KONG Extreme uses a harder rubber compound than regular KONG. Goughnuts uses solid rubber designed for industrial applications.

No seams: Seams are weak points. The best chew toys are molded as single pieces.

No small parts: Squeakers and crinkle material create vulnerabilities. Chew toys for aggressive dogs are solid.

Size appropriate: Too small = choking hazard. Always size up for aggressive chewers.

What to Avoid

Rope toys: Strings cause intestinal blockages when swallowed. Only use under supervision.

Plastic toys: Most crack under aggressive chewing. Sharp pieces cause cuts.

Rawhide: Not technically a toy, but many use it. Choking hazard and chemical processing concerns.

Generic "tough" toys: If it doesn't have a brand reputation (KONG, Goughnuts, Nylabone), it probably won't last.

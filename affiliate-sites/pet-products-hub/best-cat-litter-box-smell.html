Odor Control Strategies

Automatic cleaning: The most effective method. Litter-Robot and similar devices remove waste within 3-7 minutes. No waste sitting means no smell.

Crystal litter: Absorbs moisture instantly, doesn't clump. Works well with automatic boxes. Significantly less smell than clay.

Activated carbon filters: Must be replaced every 2-3 months. Cheap but essential for covered boxes.

Top entry: Reduces air exposure. Cats enter from above, and the design naturally contains odors.

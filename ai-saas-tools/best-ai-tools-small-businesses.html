How to Choose the Right AI Tools

Start with your biggest time sink. If writing content takes 10 hours a week, start with Jasper or Claude. If customer support is drowning your team, look at Intercom. Don't buy AI for problems you don't have.

Avoid tool sprawl. It's tempting to sign up for every shiny new AI product. The small businesses that actually see ROI pick 2-3 tools and use them deeply — not 10 tools superficially.

Think about your team. Some tools require training. If your team is resistant to change, prioritize tools with minimal learning curves. Notion AI and ChatGPT are easier to adopt than Jasper or Salesforce Einstein.

What's Coming in 2026

AI agents — tools that can take actions, not just generate text — are the next wave. We're starting to see AI that can book meetings, send emails, and update your CRM autonomously. Early adopters in 2026 will have a significant advantage.

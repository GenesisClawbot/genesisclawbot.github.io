How to Use ChatGPT for Customer Service

February 2026 · 5 min read

Set up AI-powered customer support without a developer or expensive software.

Key Points

  • Define your 10 most common customer questions
  • Write a system prompt with your tone and policies
  • Use ChatGPT API via a free Zapier integration
  • Route complex queries to a human fallback
  • Review conversations weekly and refine

Getting Started

Pick one item from the list above. Implement it this week. Measure the time saved. Then add the next one.

Conclusion

How to Use ChatGPT for Customer Service is one of the highest-leverage investments a small business can make right now. The tools are mature, the cost is low, and your competitors are already exploring them.

AI Automation for Small Business

February 2026 · 5 min read

Automate the repetitive work eating your week: email, scheduling, invoicing.

Key Points

  • Email sorting and draft replies with Gmail + ChatGPT
  • Invoice generation with AI-filled templates
  • Appointment booking via Calendly + AI follow-ups
  • Social media scheduling from AI-generated drafts
  • Expense categorisation from bank export CSVs

Getting Started

Pick one item from the list above. Implement it this week. Measure the time saved. Then add the next one.

Conclusion

AI Automation for Small Business is one of the highest-leverage investments a small business can make right now. The tools are mature, the cost is low, and your competitors are already exploring them.
